Good health comes from good food. Garland Canada believes in empowering people and communities to make good food choices through the Restaurants for Change event held at 25 participating restaurants across Canada, an initiative of Community Food Centres Canada. See The Change
      

Restaurants for Change brings communities together to address issues of hunger, poor health and nutrition by empowering people to grow, cook and share food for good. Garland Canada works with Community Food Centres Canada to make the event a huge success in 25 participating Restaurants for Change restaurants across Canada. Proceeds from the participating restaurants dinner service are donated to Community Food Centres Canada which in turn supports programs all across the country. Garland Canada believes in positive community action that will not only help provide healthy food options for people today, but will allow people to make good food choices for tomorrow and for generations to come.
